## Summary

TimeCue does not collect data from users.

The app stores your cue sentences, cue deck, cue logs, reminder schedule, repeat choice, and notification style locally on your device so it can show your saved cues and schedule local notifications. The developer does not receive, collect, track, sell, or share this information.

## Information We Collect

TimeCue does not collect personal information, usage analytics, device identifiers, contact information, health information, location data, or advertising data.

The following settings may be stored locally on your device:

- Your cue sentences and saved cue deck
- Cue log entries you create inside the app
- Fixed reminder times you enable
- A custom reminder date, time, and repeat choice
- Your selected notification style
- Notification permission state as reported by iOS

These settings are used only by the app on your device.

## How We Use Information

TimeCue uses locally stored settings to:

- Show your current cue inside the app
- Show saved cues, templates you have added, and local cue logs
- Schedule local notifications at the times you choose
- Let you send a 10-second test cue
- Let notification actions such as Later create a follow-up cue

The developer does not have access to your cue sentences, cue deck, cue logs, or schedule.

## Permissions

TimeCue asks for notification permission so it can deliver your cue sentence at the scheduled time.

If you do not grant notification permission, you can still edit your cue inside the app, but TimeCue cannot deliver scheduled cue notifications.

## Third-Party Services

TimeCue does not include third-party analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, tracking SDKs, or developer-operated cloud services.

The app is distributed through Apple's App Store. Apple may process purchase, download, crash, or diagnostic information under Apple's own terms and privacy policy.

## Data Sharing

TimeCue does not sell, rent, trade, or share user data with third parties.

## Data Retention and Deletion

Cue settings, saved cues, and cue logs remain on your device until you edit them, clear them, or delete the app. Removing TimeCue from your device deletes its locally stored app settings according to iOS behavior.

You can also manage or disable TimeCue notifications in iOS Settings.

## Children

TimeCue is a general productivity reminder app. It is not designed to collect information from children, and the developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Medical and Emergency Disclaimer

TimeCue is a general personal reminder tool. It is not therapy, medical advice, crisis support, or emergency support.

If you are in immediate danger or need urgent help, contact local emergency services or a qualified support provider.

## Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated when TimeCue changes. The effective date above will be updated when material changes are made.
