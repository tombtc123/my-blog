<h2>Made for the part learners actually repeat</h2>
<p>KanaFlow Practice does not try to become a full Japanese course. It focuses on the small loop that makes early reading feel automatic: recognition, recall, feedback, and repetition.</p>
<p>The app accepts common romaji variants such as <code>si</code> for <code>shi</code>, trims extra spaces, and plays the kana aloud after each answer so visual memory and sound stay connected.</p>
</div>
<div class="kf-panel">
<h3>Practice flow</h3>
<ol>

<li>Use the saved mistake list to revisit only weak characters.</li>
</ol>
